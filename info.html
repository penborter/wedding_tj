Details

Date: Sunday 4th October 2026

Ceremony: Please arrive at 2:45pm for a 3:00pm ceremony start at Palm House at the Royal Botanic Gardens. The ceremony will go for approximately 30 minutes. Following the ceremony, there will be an opportunity for photos in the gardens.

Reception: from 5:00pm at Nomad, Surry Hills

Travel

Palm House is located in the Royal Botanic Gardens, around a 3-5 minutes' walk from any entrance.

Metro: Martin Place Station

Train: Circular Quay Station

Uber/Taxi: Best drop-off points are Macquarie Street or Mrs Macquarie Road

If you get lost, don’t worry, there are plenty of signs in the gardens to guide you there!

After the ceremony we will make our way to Nomad together — and by together, we mean all of us! In true Tammy and Jono style, we would love for you to join us on the Metro to Central station. The photographer will be coming along with us and we will walk to the restaurant together.

Dress

The dress code is: COCKTAIL!

Think spring, colourful, floral and fun! We know you all own fabulous dresses, skirts, suits, shirts and ties, so please wear them! We’d love for you to wear something you already own.

FAQs

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by August 4th, so we can have an accurate headcount.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Please let us know in your RSVP so we can make sure you're covered.

Are we allowed to take our own photos?

Take as many pictures as you wish during the day! We do ask you don't take any during the ceremony. We have an excellent photographer that will be able to capture these moments. Please upload your photos and videos here.

Is there a registry?

Your presence the greatest gift to us. There will be a wishing well at the reception if you'd like to contribute to our future together.

Is the wedding indoors?

The ceremony will be outdoors, with a wet weather option available. As you’ll be walking through the gardens, we recommend bringing an umbrella if rain is forecast. We’ll provide a weather update 3 days before the wedding. (you can’t trust the weather more than 3 days out).

When does the event conclude?

The reception concludes at 10pm. If you’d like to continue the celebrations, we’re in the heart of the city — and Tio’s is just next door!

Any other questions?

If you have any more questions, feel free to give either of us a call!
