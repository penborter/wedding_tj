You can get to the gardens via Circular Quay, so whether you’re coming by train, ferry or light rail you are sorted! Head left with the harbour behind you, and take the stairs up to Macquarie Street (these are located on the right-hand side of Baskin Robbins). For those taking the Metro: we recommend getting off at Martin Place and walking up the hill to Macquarie St entrance For those using Uber: you can get these from Macquarie Street or Mrs Macquarie road! FAQs

When is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by August 4th, so we can have an accurate headcount.

Will there be any Jewish traditions?

There will be Jewish dancing at Nomad! We will be able guide you as to how this works and our friends and family will be able to assist. There are no wrong dance moves here, take someone’s hand and join the circle! If you have questions, reach out.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

Please let us know in your RSVP so we can make sure you're covered.

Are we allowed to take our own pictures?

Take as many pictures as you wish during the day! We do ask you don't take any during the ceremony. We have an excellent photographer that will be able to capture these photos. We will have a link to upload photos on the website.

Is there a registry?

You are the greatest gift we have! There will be a wishing well at the reception if you feel so inclined to contribute to our future.

Is the wedding indoors?

The ceremony will be outdoors (with a wet weather option ). You will be walking outside through the gardens to get to the ceremony, so if it does rain, it will be best to bring an umbrella. We will provide an update 3 days prior to the wedding on this (you can’t trust the weather more than 3 days out).

When does the event conclude?

10pm! However if you wish to continue the night, we are located in the heart of the city, and Tios is just next door.

Any other questions?

If you have any more questions, feel free to give either of us a call!
